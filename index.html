What Is The Fair Meds Act?

FAIR stands for Fairness in Access and Insurance for Required Medicines.

The FAIR Meds Act opens communication between a patient and pharmacist without restriction, allows pharmacy access for patients without the pharmacy paying fees for participation or transmittal of Prescription claims, empowers the Department of Insurance to better enforce the Pharmacy Audit Integrity Act and allows employers the full benefits of any rebates generated by their employeesâ€™ drug claims and retains the ownership of drug data files.

Bottom line: it protects patients, provides more information and benefits to employer paid health plans, and allows your pharmacy to answer your questions about drugs and work better with you.

Patients and employers deserve the ability to have truth and transparency in prescription drug pricing.
